## How to Play Word Wipe

Word Wipe is a fast-paced, grid-based puzzle game that blends the mechanics of Boggle with the satisfying physics of a match-three game. To succeed in Word Wipe, you must use your vocabulary and spatial awareness to clear the board before the clock runs out.

### Basic Mechanics and Controls
The Word Wipe game presents you with a 10x10 grid filled with random letter tiles. Unlike many word games that restrict movement to horizontal or vertical lines, Word Wipe allows for total freedom. You can connect letters in any direction—vertically, horizontally, or diagonally—within the Word Wipe interface.

To play Word Wipe, simply click and hold your mouse (or press and drag on mobile) over the first letter of your chosen word. Drag your cursor through the adjacent letters to highlight the word, then release to submit it to the Word Wipe engine. If the word is valid (minimum of three characters), the tiles will vanish from the Word Wipe board. 

### The Gravity Effect
What sets Word Wipe apart is its dynamic grid. When letters are removed in Word Wipe, the tiles positioned above them drop down to fill the empty spaces, much like a Tetris-style collapse. This "wiping" mechanic in Word Wipe constantly rearranges the board, creating new opportunities for word combinations but also requiring you to think several steps ahead to ensure you don't strand useful letters in your Word Wipe session.

### Objectives and Special Tools
The primary goal of each Word Wipe level is to clear a specific number of lines from the grid within a strict time limit. You clear a line in Word Wipe by removing all the tiles within a horizontal row or vertical column. As you progress through the Word Wipe levels, the quota for cleared lines increases, demanding faster reflexes and a sharper eye for the Word Wipe grid.

To help you when the Word Wipe grid becomes cluttered, the game rewards your progress with "Bombs." Once earned in Word Wipe, you can place a bomb on any section of the grid to clear a small area of tiles instantly. This is a vital tactical tool for removing difficult letters like 'Q' or 'Z' that might be preventing you from clearing a final Word Wipe row. Keep an eye on the Word Wipe timer at the bottom of the screen; if it hits zero before you reach your goal, the Word Wipe game is over!

## Tips & Strategies for Word Wipe

To excel at Word Wipe, you need more than just a large vocabulary; you need speed and spatial awareness. Use these professional Word Wipe strategies to clear lines faster and achieve higher scores.

*   **Prioritize Vertical Columns over Horizontal Rows**
    While the goal of Word Wipe is to clear lines, focusing on vertical stacks often yields better results. Clearing letters vertically in Word Wipe causes more tiles to drop, which reshuffles the Word Wipe board more effectively and creates fresh opportunities for high-value words.
*   **Target the Bottom of the Grid First**
    Whenever possible in Word Wipe, look for words near the bottom of the board. Removing these Word Wipe tiles causes the greatest shift in the letter layout above. This "shuffling" effect in Word Wipe can often result in accidental word alignments that you can capitalize on immediately.
*   **Hunt for Common Prefixes and Suffixes**
    To speed up your Word Wipe gameplay, look for common letter clusters like "-ING," "-ED," "-TION," or "PRE-." Because you can connect letters in any direction in Word Wipe (including diagonally), these common endings are often easy to find and can be expanded into longer, high-scoring Word Wipe words.
*   **Save Your Bombs for "Dead Zones"**
    The Word Wipe bombs you earn are your most valuable resource. Don’t use them as soon as they appear in Word Wipe. Instead, save them for moments when you have a "dead zone"—a section of the Word Wipe board filled with difficult letters like Q, Z, or X that are preventing you from clearing a specific line.
*   **Keep Your Eyes Moving**
    Because of the strict Word Wipe time limit, it is easy to get "tunnel vision" on one corner of the board. If you can’t find a word in Word Wipe within three seconds, shift your focus to the opposite side. Word Wipe is about momentum; it is better to submit several three-letter Word Wipe words quickly than to waste ten seconds searching for one five-letter word.
*   **Work Toward the Goal Line**
    Always keep an eye on the Word Wipe objective bar. Your primary mission is to clear full rows or columns to meet the Word Wipe level requirement. If you only need one more tile to clear a line in Word Wipe, prioritize any word—no matter how short—that includes that specific tile.
*   **Utilize Diagonal Connections**
    New Word Wipe players often forget that diagonal moves are legal. This is the key to unlocking the Word Wipe board. If you feel stuck in Word Wipe, look at the tiles diagonally; you will often find that a "U" or an "S" is reachable from a direction you hadn't previously considered.

## FAQ about Word Wipe

### How do you play Word Wipe?
To play Word Wipe, you must find and connect adjacent letter tiles on the grid to form words. You can drag your mouse or finger in any direction in Word Wipe—vertically, horizontally, or diagonally—to link letters. Once you release, the Word Wipe word is submitted; if valid, the tiles disappear, and the letters above fall into the empty Word Wipe spaces.

### What is the minimum word length required?
In Word Wipe, the minimum length for any valid word is three characters. While shorter Word Wipe words help clear specific tiles quickly, forming longer words will earn you higher Word Wipe scores and help you reach your level goals more efficiently before the timer runs out.

### How do the "Bomb" power-ups work?
Word Wipe bombs are special rewards earned after you successfully clear a required number of lines from the Word Wipe board. Once awarded, you can use a Word Wipe bomb to demolish a specific section of the grid. This is strategically useful for removing difficult letter combinations or clearing that final row needed to advance to the next Word Wipe level.

### Is there a time limit in Word Wipe?
Yes, Word Wipe is a race against the clock. Each Word Wipe level comes with a specific time limit, adding an arcade-style challenge to the Word Wipe puzzle gameplay. You must meet the Word Wipe objective of clearing the required number of lines before the timer reaches zero to progress.

### Can I play Word Wipe on mobile devices?
Absolutely! Word Wipe is designed to be a versatile casual game. It is fully optimized for mobile browsers, allowing you to drag and connect Word Wipe letters using touch controls on your smartphone or tablet, just as easily as you would with a mouse on a desktop Word Wipe session.
