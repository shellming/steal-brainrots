The secret to exponential growth lies in the **Melter and Merger** stations found in Obby Brainrot Merge. As your items pass through these machines, they are combined and leveled up. Each upgrade in Obby Brainrot Merge increases the value of the creature, meaning you earn more currency when they reach the end of the line. Your primary goal is to reinvest these profits into purchasing more advanced factory buildings and faster conveyor systems to optimize the flow of your Obby Brainrot Merge production line.
