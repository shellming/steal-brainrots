## How to Play Castle Craft

Step into the shoes of Fiona, a determined farmer on a mystical quest to find her missing husband, Michael. In *Castle Craft*, your primary objective is to dispel the fog covering the land by merging resources, completing missions, and rebuilding a grand medieval kingdom.

### Master the Merge Mechanics
The core of *Castle Craft* revolves around the "merge-3" mechanic. To progress, you must drag and drop identical items together to create more advanced versions. For example, merging three wheat sprouts will cultivate a full wheat crop. As you advance, you will merge logs to create timber, which can then be crafted into essential tools and building materials. Always look for opportunities to merge five items instead of three to receive a bonus item, maximizing your efficiency.

### Clearing the Fog and Exploring
The world is initially shrouded in a thick, magical fog. To expand your territory, you must harvest crops and gather resources to trade for **Ancient Keys**. These keys act as the primary currency for exploration, dispelling the fog and revealing hidden realms, treasure chests, and clues about Michael’s whereabouts. Each new area unlocked introduces new puzzles and historical figures who aid in your journey.

### Building and Crafting
Resource management is vital for restoring the land. You will meet allies like Aiden the woodsman, who helps clear obstacles such as stubborn tree stumps. By gathering timber and stone, you can transition from a simple garden to a bustling marketplace and eventually a grand castle. Open treasure chests found throughout the map to discover unique items and heroes that provide "Premium Perks" and speed up your restoration efforts.

### Controls
Playing *Castle Craft* is intuitive and designed for both desktop and mobile players:
*   **Merge/Move Items:** Click and drag the **left mouse button** (or tap and drag on mobile) to move items across the grid.
*   **Interact:** Click on harvested crops, treasure chests, or NPCs to collect rewards and initiate dialogue.
*   **Camera:** Click and drag on the background to pan across your growing kingdom.

## Tips & Strategies for Castle Craft

To master the mystical fog and reunite Fiona with Michael, you’ll need more than just a green thumb. Use these strategic tips to optimize your resource management and expand your kingdom efficiently.

*   **Prioritize the "Rule of Five":** While merging three items creates a superior object, merging five items at once grants you two upgraded pieces. This is the most efficient way to maximize your resources and progress through the game’s building chains much faster.
*   **Clear Fog Strategically:** Don't spend your mystical keys on just any patch of land. Look for areas that contain blocked treasure chests or essential resources like timber. Prioritizing these "high-value" zones will give you the materials needed to unlock even more keys quickly.
*   **Manage Your Board Space:** As your garden evolves into a kingdom, space becomes your most valuable asset. Keep your board organized by grouping similar items together. This prevents accidental merges and makes it easier to see when you are close to hitting a five-item bonus.
*   **Focus on Daily Missions:** Check your mission log frequently. Completing tasks for characters like Aiden often yields rare rewards and specific items required to clear major obstacles, such as stubborn tree stumps, which might otherwise halt your progress.
*   **Save Your Treasure Chests:** It can be tempting to open chests the moment you get them, but saving them for when you have a specific goal—like building a marketplace or a grand castle—ensures you have the influx of tools and heroes exactly when they are needed most.
*   **Balance Farming and Exploration:** While it’s easy to get caught up in merging wheat sprouts, remember that harvesting is a means to an end. Always keep a portion of your energy dedicated to gathering timber and stone to ensure your construction projects don't stall while your garden thrives.
