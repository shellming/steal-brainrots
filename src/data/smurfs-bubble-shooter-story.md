## Tips & Strategies for Smurfs Bubble Shooter Story

The most effective way to clear a board in **Smurfs Bubble Shooter Story** is to aim for the bubbles at the top of a cluster. When you break the "anchor" bubbles in **Smurfs Bubble Shooter Story**, all the bubbles underneath will fall, regardless of their color.
