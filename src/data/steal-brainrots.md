## How to Play Steal Brainrots

Steal Brainrots is a high-stakes social heist game where the objective is simple: accumulate the rarest collection of Italian-inspired memes and defend them at all costs. To succeed in Steal Brainrots, you must balance your roles as a collector, a thief, and a base defender.

### Getting Started: Building Your Collection
You begin your journey with a small amount of cash. Your primary goal in Steal Brainrots is to acquire **Brainrots**, such as the elusive *Tralalero Tralala* or *Cappuccino Assassino*. There are two ways to fill your base:
*   **The Red Carpet:** Purchase Brainrots directly from the conveyor belt of "meme prisoners." This is the safest way to grow your wealth in Steal Brainrots, but it requires significant capital.
*   **Raiding:** Sneak into another player’s base and snatch their Brainrots for free.

Once you have acquired a Brainrot, place it on a pillar in your base. These memes act as passive income generators; the more you display in Steal Brainrots, the faster your money grows. Every Steal Brainrots player wants the biggest collection.

### The Art of the Heist
Stealing is the most efficient way to climb the leaderboard, but it comes with immense risk. To steal in Steal Brainrots, enter an opponent's base and interact with their collection. Carrying a Brainrot back to your base triggers several penalties:
*   **Reduced Speed:** Your movement is significantly slowed in Steal Brainrots.
*   **Item Restrictions:** You cannot use boosters or items while carrying a stolen meme.
*   **Instant Alerts:** The owner is immediately notified of the theft.

If the owner catches you, they can use their hammer to knock you down, forcing you to drop the loot. **Pro Tip:** If you are cornered while raiding in Steal Brainrots, press **'G'** on your keyboard to teleport home instantly, though you will leave the stolen Brainrot behind. Successful Steal Brainrots players know when to run.

### Defending Your Wealth
As your collection grows in Steal Brainrots, you become a high-priority target. To protect your assets, you must invest in:
*   **Locks:** Physical barriers that prevent intruders from walking into your base.
*   **Shields:** Temporary protection that lasts for 60 seconds. Use these wisely during high-traffic periods in Steal Brainrots.
*   **Traps:** Hidden hazards designed to slow down or stop thieves in their tracks.

### Rebirths and Progression
Once you have accumulated enough wealth and Brainrots, you can choose to **Rebirth**. This resets your current progress in Steal Brainrots in exchange for permanent multipliers and rewards, allowing you to earn money faster and climb higher on the global leaderboard in your next run. Rebirthing is key to Steal Brainrots mastery.

## Tips & Strategies for Steal Brainrots

Steal Brainrots rewards the careful player.

Steal Brainrots players use this often.

## FAQ about Steal Brainrots

### How do I get more money to buy Brainrots?
In **Steal Brainrots**, the primary way to generate income is by placing your collected memes on the pillars inside your base. Each Brainrot you own generates a steady stream of cash over time. To maximize your earnings in Steal Brainrots, you can either purchase new memes from the "red carpet" conveyor belt or successfully raid other players' bases to expand your collection without spending your own gold.

### What happens if someone tries to steal from my base?
When a player enters your base and grabs a Brainrot, you will receive an instant alert. Because carrying a Brainrot slows the thief down and prevents them from using items in Steal Brainrots, you have a window of opportunity to defend your property. Use your hammer to knock the thief down, which forces them to drop the meme so you can return it to its pillar. Steal Brainrots defense is vital.

### How do locks and shields work?
Locks and shields are your primary defenses against intruders in Steal Brainrots. Shields provide a temporary layer of absolute protection, but they only last for 60 seconds, making them best suited for moments when you are vulnerable or away from your keyboard for a short burst. For long-term security in Steal Brainrots, investing in better locks and traps is essential to deter other players from eyeing your rare collection.

### What is a Rebirth and why should I do it?
Once you have accumulated enough wealth and Brainrots, you can choose to "Rebirth." This process resets your current progress but grants you permanent multipliers and long-term rewards in Steal Brainrots. Rebirthing is the most effective way to climb the global leaderboards, as it allows you to earn money significantly faster in your next Steal Brainrots playthrough.

### Can I teleport back home while carrying a stolen Brainrot?
No. While you can use the home button (shortcut 'G' on keyboards) to quickly escape a dangerous situation or get back to your base in Steal Brainrots, you cannot take a stolen Brainrot with you through teleportation. If you want to successfully keep a stolen meme in Steal Brainrots, you must physically carry it back to your base and place it on a pillar yourself. Steal Brainrots mechanics are very strict about this rule. This makes Steal Brainrots a very challenging game for everyone involved. Good luck with Steal Brainrots!
