## How to Play Stone Grass

Stone Grass is a satisfying simulation game that transforms the simple act of mowing lawns into a rewarding empire-building experience. To succeed, you must balance efficient field clearing with strategic resource management. Below is a breakdown of the mechanics and objectives you need to master.

### Mastering the Controls
Getting started in Stone Grass is intuitive, allowing you to focus on the satisfying visuals of cutting grass. You can guide your mower using:
*   **Keyboard:** Use the **WASD** keys or **Arrow Keys** to steer your vehicle in any direction.
*   **Mouse/Touch:** Click and drag (or slide your finger) to lead the mower across the overgrown patches of your virtual farms.

The movement system is physics-based and smooth, meaning you can create precise lines or circular paths to ensure every blade is collected efficiently.

### The Gameplay Loop: Cut, Collect, and Sell
The core of every playthrough revolves around a three-step cycle designed for maximum relaxation and incremental growth:

1.  **Mowing and Clearing:** Maneuver your machine through the dense, wild fields. As you move, your mower automatically cuts the long grass, leaving a clean trail behind you. Your primary objective is full 100% field completion.
2.  **Storage Management:** As you cut, the harvest is automatically bundled and stored in your mower’s hopper. Keep a close eye on your capacity meter; once it is full, your mower will stop collecting until you empty it.
3.  **Market Delivery:** Drive your loaded mower to the designated market drop-off point. Here, your grass clippings are instantly converted into coins, the primary currency used for all progression.

### Upgrading and Expansion
As you earn coins by playing, you can visit the upgrade shop to enhance your overall efficiency. Focus on **Cutting Power** to handle tougher vegetation, **Movement Speed** to clear fields faster, and **Storage Capacity** to minimize the number of trips back to the market.

Once a field is fully cleared, you will unlock new, larger zones. These areas offer denser grass and unique layouts, providing a steady challenge as you work toward building the ultimate farming empire. Success in Stone Grass is all about finding your perfect flow—balancing the path of your mower with the timing of your upgrades.

## Tips & Strategies for Stone Grass

Mastering the art of the perfect mow in Stone Grass requires more than just driving in circles. To build your Stone Grass farming empire efficiently and maximize your earnings, follow these essential strategies:

### Prioritize Capacity Upgrades Early
In the early stages of Stone Grass, your biggest bottleneck is the overall size of your storage hopper. Frequent trips to the delivery point break your flow and waste time. Focus your first few sets of coins from Stone Grass on increasing your **storage capacity**. This allows you to clear larger patches of the field in a single pass, significantly increasing your uptime on the grass.

### Master the Spiral Pattern
To ensure you aren't leaving behind small, unsightly tufts of foliage that require backtracking, adopt a spiral pattern in Stone Grass. Start from the outer edges of a zone and work your way toward the center. This method keeps your mower constantly engaged with uncut grass and minimizes the time spent traveling over already cleared sections of Stone Grass.

### Balance Speed and Power in Stone Grass
While it is tempting to put all your resources into movement speed, don't neglect your **cutting power** in Stone Grass. As you unlock new Stone Grass environments, the fields become denser and noticeably tougher to manage. If your power is too low, your mower will slow down significantly when hitting thick patches. Keep your speed and power upgrades relatively balanced to maintain a consistent, satisfying pace throughout Stone Grass.

### Optimize Your Route to the Market in Stone Grass
Efficiency in Stone Grass isn't just about how you harvest, but how you travel. Always try to end your storage-filling run near the delivery point. By planning your path so that your bag hits maximum capacity right as you reach the drop-off zone, you eliminate unnecessary travel time across the map in Stone Grass.

### Clear Difficult Obstacles First in Stone Grass
Some fields in Stone Grass contain specific obstacles or irregular terrain that can disrupt your forward movement. Spend the first minute of your Stone Grass session clearing the grass tightly around these obstacles. Once those difficult areas are managed, you can enjoy a long, uninterrupted flow across the open sections of the field without worrying about getting stuck or losing your momentum in Stone Grass.

### Invest in Efficiency for Long-Term Gains in Stone Grass
Before expanding to the most expensive new zones in Stone Grass, ensure your current equipment is high-performing. It is often better to fully upgrade your mower on an easier field than to struggle with underpowered equipment in a dense, advanced area. A highly efficient mower makes the transition to new, larger environments much smoother and more rewarding in Stone Grass.

## FAQ about Stone Grass

### How do I upgrade my equipment in Stone Grass?
Upgrading is essential for managing larger fields in Stone Grass. You can improve your mower’s speed, cutting power, and storage capacity by visiting the in-game shop. Use the coins earned from selling clippings to purchase these enhancements, which allow you to clear denser patches and reduce the frequency of trips to the market in Stone Grass.

### What is the primary objective of the Stone Grass game?
The main goal of Stone Grass is to transform overgrown, wild fields into tidy, beautifully managed landscapes. By mowing effectively, collecting resources, and selling them for profit, you unlock new areas and more challenging environments, steadily building your Stone Grass agricultural empire.

### Can I play Stone Grass with a controller or mouse?
Stone Grass features a highly flexible and intuitive control scheme. You can guide your mower using the WASD keys or arrow keys on your keyboard. Alternatively, for a more casual experience in Stone Grass, you can use mouse dragging to navigate the fields with perfect precision.

### How do I unlock new areas to mow in Stone Grass?
New zones in Stone Grass become accessible once you have successfully cleared the current field and met specific resource requirements. As you progress, you will unlock environments with larger layouts and tougher varieties of grass that require more advanced upgrades to handle efficiently in Stone Grass.

### Is there a limit to how much grass I can carry in Stone Grass?
Yes, your mower has a specific storage capacity for clippings in Stone Grass. Once your storage is completely full, you must head to a designated market drop-off point to convert your haul into coins. Upgrading your capacity is a great way to stay in the field longer and improve your overall efficiency when playing Stone Grass.
