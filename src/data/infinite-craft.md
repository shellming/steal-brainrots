## How to Play Infinite Craft

The objective of Infinite Craft is simple yet profound: start with the four basic building blocks of the universe and combine them to discover everything from cellular life and mythical creatures to pop culture icons and celestial bodies in Infinite Craft.

### The Core Mechanics: Drag, Drop, and Discover
The gameplay of Infinite Craft revolves around a seamless "drag-and-drop" interface. On your Infinite Craft sidebar, you will find your library of discovered elements. To begin crafting in Infinite Craft:
1. **Select an Element:** Click and drag an icon (starting with Fire, Water, Earth, or Wind) onto the main Infinite Craft workspace.
2. **The Merge:** Drag a second element and drop it directly on top of the first within Infinite Craft.
3. **The Result:** If the Infinite Craft combination is valid, the two items will merge into a brand-new element, which is then permanently added to your Infinite Craft sidebar for future use.

The logic behind these Infinite Craft combinations ranges from the literal to the abstract. For instance, merging **Water + Water** creates a **Lake**, while **Earth + Fire** results in **Lava**. As you progress through Infinite Craft, you will move beyond nature into the realms of technology, philosophy, and fiction.

### Mastering the Controls and Interface
Infinite Craft is designed for accessibility, requiring only a mouse or touchscreen to play. 
*   **The Workspace:** This is your infinite canvas in the Infinite Craft interface. You can clear the screen at any time using the "Reset" or "Clear" function if it becomes too cluttered with Infinite Craft experiments.
*   **Duplication:** You can pull multiple instances of the same element onto the Infinite Craft screen to create advanced tiers (e.g., combining two Lakes to form an Ocean in Infinite Craft).
*   **The Hint System:** If you find yourself hitting a creative block in Infinite Craft, look for the light bulb icon in the bottom right corner. This Infinite Craft feature provides a nudge in the right direction to help you unlock the next stage of your universe.

### Progression and Ranks
The Infinite Craft game tracks your progress through a tier-based ranking system. As you expand your Infinite Craft library, you will climb from **Bronze** to **Silver**, and eventually **Gold** status. Each rank in Infinite Craft requires a specific number of unique discoveries. In Infinite Craft, "stacking" the same element often leads to a larger version of itself—Water plus Water creates a Lake, while two Lakes form an Ocean. This is the fastest way to unlock foundational geographical markers in Infinite Craft.

Instead of randomly dragging icons in Infinite Craft, focus on a specific category. If you want to reach "Humanity" in Infinite Craft, try focusing on combining biological elements like Life, Earth, and Time. If you are aiming for "Technology," focus on Fire, Metal, and Electricity. ### Clear Your Workspace Regularly
