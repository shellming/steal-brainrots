## Survive and Thrive in Raft Life

Raft Life is an addictive simulation game where you transform a tiny wooden plank into a massive, profitable floating empire amidst the deep blue sea.

### The Ultimate Oceanic Farming Adventure

Forget traditional countryside landscapes; **Raft Life** takes the farming tycoon genre to the open ocean. In this unique management sim, you begin your journey with nothing but an ax and a few scraps of driftwood. What starts as a desperate struggle for survival quickly evolves into a sophisticated business operation. By scavenging floating debris and mastering the art of maritime agriculture, you will learn to cultivate crops like corn and wheat while expanding your territory to accommodate livestock and industrial warehouses.

This game perfectly blends the relaxing pace of an idle clicker with the strategic depth of a resource management simulator. Whether you are defending your hard-earned progress from hungry sharks or optimizing your supply chain to maximize gold production, every decision impacts your growth. If you have ever dreamed of building a self-sustaining ecosystem from scratch, it is time to step onto the deck and start your digital agricultural legacy.

## How to Play Raft Life

Raft Life combines the relaxing nature of a farming simulator with the strategic depth of an incremental tycoon game. Your primary objective is to transform a tiny wooden platform into a massive, self-sustaining maritime empire. By balancing resource gathering, agricultural production, and defensive upgrades, you can dominate the open seas.

### Mastering the Basics and Controls
Getting started in Raft Life is intuitive, allowing you to focus on growth rather than complex inputs. You control your character using the **arrow keys** or **WASD**. The game utilizes a "proximity-based action" system: simply walk up to any interactable station—such as a plot of soil, a tree, or a construction site—and your character will automatically begin performing the necessary task. Whether you are chopping wood with your starter ax or harvesting ripe crops, your avatar handles the labor as long as you stay in position.

### Developing Your Agricultural Supply Chain
The core gameplay loop revolves around the conversion of raw materials into profit.
*   **Scavenging:** Your journey begins by collecting floating debris and wood. These are the essential building blocks for expanding your surface area.
*   **Farming and Livestock:** Once you have enough space, transition into a producer. Plant high-yield crops like corn and wheat to generate immediate trade value. As you progress, invest in livestock. Raising chickens for eggs and cows for milk provides a steady stream of "passive" income that is vital for late-game upgrades.
*   **Expansion:** Use your accumulated wealth to unlock new zones on your raft. Each expansion offers space for specialized infrastructure, such as warehouses for storage or floating markets to optimize your sales.

### Managing Risks and Efficiency
Survival isn't guaranteed on the high seas. You must remain vigilant against environmental hazards and the ever-present threat of sharks, which can damage your infrastructure. To reach "Tycoon" status, you must optimize your production lines, ensuring that your farming and animal husbandry efforts work in harmony to maximize every square inch of your floating paradise.

## Tips & Strategies for Raft Life

To transform your humble wooden plank into a sprawling oceanic empire, you need more than just luck; you need a solid plan. Use these professional strategies to maximize your efficiency and protect your resources in Raft Life.

### Prioritize Automated Resource Collection
In the early game, your manual labor is your most valuable asset. Focus on collecting floating debris immediately to expand your raft's footprint. The larger your surface area, the more "passive" catch points you create for drifting materials, allowing you to spend less time chasing individual planks and more time managing your crops.

### Master the Crop Rotation for Quick Cash
Not all crops are created equal. While it may be tempting to hoard resources, the fastest way to grow is to sell your initial harvests of corn and wheat immediately. Reinvest this gold into higher-tier seeds as soon as they become available. This "snowball effect" ensures that your income scales faster than your expansion costs.

### Build Defensive Perimeters Against Sharks
Sharks are the primary threat to your infrastructure. Instead of reacting to attacks, be proactive by reinforcing the outer edges of your raft first. By upgrading the durability of your perimeter tiles, you buy yourself precious time to fend off predators before they can destroy your expensive machinery or livestock pens.

### Optimize Your Walking Paths
Efficiency is key in management sims. Arrange your raft so that your most frequent tasks—such as harvesting water and tending to livestock—are located centrally or close to one another. Minimizing the travel time between your storage crates and your production zones will significantly increase your "units produced per minute."

### Invest in Livestock Early
While farming provides quick cash, livestock like chickens and cows offer a consistent, long-term revenue stream that requires less constant clicking. Aim to unlock your first animal pen as soon as your basic food supply is stabilized. The passive income generated from eggs and milk allows you to focus on exploring new building upgrades.

### Balance Expansion with Infrastructure
It is easy to get carried away with making a massive raft, but a large raft is useless if you don't have the warehouses to store your goods. Always ensure your storage capacity stays one step ahead of your production. There is nothing more wasteful in Raft Life than harvesting a high-value crop only to realize you have no room to keep it!

## FAQ about Raft Life

### How do I defend my farm against shark attacks?
In Raft Life, sharks are a constant threat to your floating empire. To protect your resources, you must stay vigilant and use your gathered materials to reinforce the edges of your raft. As you progress and upgrade your infrastructure, you will unlock better defensive tools to keep these aquatic predators at bay and ensure your crops and livestock remain safe.

### What is the fastest way to earn money in the game?
The most efficient way to generate income is by optimizing your supply chain. Start by focusing on high-yield crops like wheat and corn, then reinvest those profits into livestock like chickens and cows. Processing raw materials into secondary goods and selling them at floating markets will significantly increase your profit margins compared to selling basic resources.

### Can I automate tasks in Raft Life?
Yes! As an incremental management game, Raft Life allows you to streamline your production. By approaching active locations, your character automatically performs tasks like chopping wood or harvesting crops. As you expand your raft and upgrade your facilities, your "digital agricultural tycoon" becomes more efficient, allowing you to focus on large-scale expansion rather than manual labor.

### How do I expand the size of my raft?
To grow your raft, you must consistently collect floating debris such as wood and leaves using your ax. Once you have accumulated enough resources, you can access the construction menu to build new platforms. Expanding is essential for unlocking advanced features like warehouses and larger animal pens.

### What happens if the weather conditions become harsh?
Harsh climatic conditions act as a management challenge that can slow down your production. During these periods, it is vital to have a well-organized infrastructure and stored resources in your warehouses. Managing these risks effectively is what separates a novice player from a true tycoon in the Raft Life ecosystem.
