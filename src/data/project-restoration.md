## Welcome to Project Restoration

Project Restoration is a cozy post-apocalyptic farm-adventure where you help three robots revive a forgotten Earth.

### A New Beginning for a Forgotten World
Set 200 years after the disappearance of humanity, Project Restoration invites players into a vibrant world where nature and decay coexist. You take control of three charming robot heroes who awaken to a silent planet, tasked with the monumental goal of rebuilding civilization from the ground up. This isn't your typical bleak wasteland; instead, it is a lush, secret-filled environment waiting for a touch of warmth and a bit of mechanical ingenuity to flourish once again.

### Farm, Build, and Uncover the Truth
Blending classic farming mechanics with deep exploration, the game challenges you to grow crops, manage resources, and construct intricate workshops across 56 unique locations. From the depths of radioactive forests to the rusted hulls of crashed starships, every area offers over 1,000 quests filled with puzzles and lore. Whether you are decorating your base with vintage steampunk gear or collaborating with friends in multiplayer events, Project Restoration offers a heartwarming and management-rich experience for fans of the genre.

## How to Play Project Restoration

In *Project Restoration*, you take on the role of a guide for three charming robots awakening to a world reclaimed by nature and mystery. Your primary objective is to transform a decaying post-apocalyptic wasteland into a thriving, eco-friendly civilization. By blending traditional farming mechanics with deep exploration, the game challenges you to heal the Earth while uncovering the secrets of why humanity vanished 200 years ago.

### Master the Core Gameplay Loop
The gameplay revolves around a cycle of resource management and expansion. You begin by clearing debris to make room for crops and essential workshops. As you harvest organic materials and scavenge for scrap metal, you can fulfill quests for the 30 unique NPCs scattered across 56 diverse biomes. Progressing through the story requires balancing your farm’s productivity with the exploration of dangerous ruins, such as radioactive forests and crashed starships, to find rare blueprints and vintage furnishings.

### Intuitive Controls for Every Platform
Whether you are playing on PC or mobile, the controls are designed for a seamless, "cozy" experience.

*   **PC Controls:** Use the **Left-click** to interact with the world, harvest crops, and move your robots. To customize your base, **Hold Left-click** on any building or decor item to enter Edit Mode, allowing for free-form repositioning. **Dragging the mouse** while clicking allows you to pan across the vast map.
*   **Mobile Controls:** A **Single tap** handles all primary interactions, from initiating dialogue to starting production in workshops. To rearrange your settlement, **Long-press** on an object to activate Edit Mode. Navigation is handled via simple **Swipes** to scroll through menus or pan the camera.

### Building and Socializing
As you reclaim the land, use the "Edit Mode" to personalize your base with hundreds of steampunk and retro-themed decorations. Don't forget the social aspect; you can visit neighbors' farms, trade rare gifts, and participate in cooperative events to speed up your restoration efforts and bring warmth back to the world.

## Tips & Strategies for Project Restoration

To help you transform the desolate wasteland into a thriving paradise, we’ve compiled several essential strategies for your first few hours in *Project Restoration*. Use these tips to optimize your resource management and uncover the mysteries of the past more efficiently.

### Prioritize Resource Loops
In the early game, your primary goal should be establishing a steady flow of basic materials. Always keep your workshops running, even if you don't have an immediate quest requirement. Materials like scrap metal and treated wood are foundational for almost every mid-game upgrade. By keeping a surplus, you’ll avoid "bottlenecking" when a high-tier quest suddenly demands a large quantity of refined resources.

### Master the Edit Mode for Layout Efficiency
Don't just place buildings where they fit; place them where they work. Use the **hold left-click (or long-tap on mobile)** function to enter Edit Mode and reorganize your base frequently. Grouping your most-used production buildings near your central hub minimizes the amount of panning and clicking required to collect finished goods, saving you significant time during long play sessions.

### Leverage the Trio’s Unique Skills
The three robot heroes aren't just cosmetic; they represent different facets of the restoration process. Pay close attention to which robot is best suited for specific tasks. Some quests may involve complex puzzles in bunkers that require a specific hero's logic, while clearing radioactive forests might be faster with another. Rotate your focus between them to ensure their individual skill trees develop at a balanced pace.

### Explore Ancient Ruins for Rare Blueprints
While farming is the heart of the game, exploration is its soul. Don't stay confined to your starting plot for too long. Venturing into the 56 vibrant locations, such as the crashed starship or the icy mazes, is the only way to find "Ancient Technology" blueprints. These unique items unlock high-efficiency vintage furnishings and steampunk gear that provide better passive bonuses than standard craftables.

### Be a Good Neighbor
*Project Restoration* features a robust social system that shouldn't be ignored. Visit your friends’ bases daily to trade gifts and observe their layouts. Cooperative events often offer exclusive rewards that are difficult to obtain solo. Trading surplus crops for rare components with other players is often the fastest way to complete the more demanding "Megacity" reconstruction quests.

### Clear Pests Immediately
As you revive nature, you will occasionally attract mechanical pests or invasive species that can stall your production. Check your active crops and workshops regularly. Dealing with these nuisances as soon as they appear prevents them from spreading to adjacent plots, ensuring your resource generation remains at 100% efficiency.

## FAQ about Project Restoration

### What is the main objective in Project Restoration?
In Project Restoration, you lead a trio of charming robots on a mission to revive a desolate Earth 200 years after humanity’s disappearance. Your primary goals include restoring nature by growing crops, rebuilding civilization through workshop construction, and venturing into 56 unique locations to uncover the mystery of what happened to the human race.

### Can I play Project Restoration with my friends?
Yes! Project Restoration features robust multiplayer elements. You can visit your neighbors' bases to see their custom layouts, exchange helpful gifts to speed up progress, and participate in cooperative events to tackle challenges together.

### How do I move buildings or decorations once they are placed?
Repositioning your base is simple and flexible. On PC, just hold the **Left-click** on any object to enter Edit Mode. If you are playing on a mobile device, **long-press** on the building or decor item you wish to move. Once Edit Mode is active, you can freely drag the item to its new location.

### What kind of environments will I explore during the adventure?
The game features a vast world with 56 vibrant locations. You will journey through diverse biomes ranging from radioactive forests and icy mazes to crumbling megacities and underground bunkers. You will even have the chance to explore a crashed starship as you progress through the 1,000+ available quests.

### Is there a limit to how I can customize my base?
The game offers extensive creative freedom with hundreds of décor options. You can style your settlement using everything from steampunk-inspired machinery to cozy vintage furnishings and retro lamps. With no rigid placement rules in Edit Mode, you can design a post-apocalyptic sanctuary that perfectly reflects your personal style.
